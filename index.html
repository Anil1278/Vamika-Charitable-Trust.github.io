About Us

Vamika is not a name it's a process to polish a future generation. We are here to bring smile to future of India. We are with them as backbone of the body. Vamika is always trying to maintain peace and prosperity in everyone's life. On the ongoing era the human being moving towards materialistic thoughts while Vamika will always try to build a moral thoughts within them.

 

There are plenty of citizens who are not getting proper diet while vamika will try to provide a food for atleast twice per day or twice a day. Ongoing the Govt. of India is providing various polices for betterment of the citizens as well as aspirational districts but the benefit is not achieved by the people so Vamika will be the route to achieve the goal. On the 4 industrial revolution era the plenty of people migrating from one place to another for the betterment of the life. They were cutting the trees for settlement but on this time they were making their own house while they were destroying another houses such as animals. Vamika will try to gather maximum animals and try to provide a better life to them.

  Every area has certain diseases which are affecting the people's life for example the region of South Gujarat is maximum Prone to Thalassemia we will try to organize maximum camp for testing of thalassemia and we will try to bring smile to everyone's life by providing a treatment to them.

 

As the technology expanding the education is moving towards an e-learning it may create an atmosphere of discrimination for less privileged students.Or it can make a discriminating atmosphere. Suppose the child of Urban area has technology while the Rural area is devoid of technology then the rural area child will deprived of education it would can create a topic of discrimination basis. Vamika will try to avoid the discrimination basis while providing the technology equipments to the Future of India. As per the constitution of India the Article 21(A) it states the right to education. So vamika will try to maintain an equality within all of them. We have been started the Battle of non-violence for the betterment and Soverignity of India. We cannot win the battle of non-violence with 5 to 6 people we required the Support of more than 130 crore to win this battle. Your support will provide an motive to tackle any challenge in the Battle. So Praise and Support Vamikians.

-VAMIKA CHARITABLE TRUST
